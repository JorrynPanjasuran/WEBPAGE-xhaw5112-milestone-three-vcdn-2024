Welcome to Empowering the Nation!

Empowering the Nation is a skills training initiative founded by Precious Radebe. We provide training for domestic workers and gardeners, helping them gain valuable skills to enhance their employment opportunities or start their own businesses. Our courses are designed to upskill individuals, ensuring they can secure better wages and long-term career success.

Whether you're an employer looking to upskill your employees or an individual eager to enhance your skills, Empowering the Nation offers a variety of programs that meet your needs.

Available Courses

We offer both six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes, available in Johannesburg. Our courses include:

  • First Aid
  • Sewing
  • Landscaping
  • Life Skills

Short courses:

  • Child Minding
  • Cooking
  • Garden Maintenance

Join Our Growing Community

Since our establishment in 2018, we have empowered hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners to gain essential skills for better employment opportunities. By selecting multiple courses, you can benefit from our discount system:

  • One course – no discount
  • Two courses – 5% discount
  • Three courses – 10% discount
  • More than three courses – 15% discount
