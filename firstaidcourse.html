First Aid Course

Course Overview

Our First Aid Course provides essential skills to handle emergency situations effectively. This course is ideal for those looking to gain practical knowledge and certification in first aid, ensuring safety in various environments.

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Modules: Basic First Aid, CPR, Choking Relief, Wound Care
  • Schedule: Mondays and Wednesdays
  • Requirements: None, open to all levels

What You Will Learn

  • How to perform CPR and handle choking emergencies
  • Basic wound care techniques and infection control
  • Recognizing and responding to various medical emergencies
