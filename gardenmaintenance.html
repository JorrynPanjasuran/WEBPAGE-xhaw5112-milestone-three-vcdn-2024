Garden Maintenance Course

Course Overview

The Garden Maintenance Course equips learners with essential skills to maintain and enhance garden landscapes. Whether for personal gardens or working in the industry, this course provides practical knowledge in plant care, soil management, landscaping, and garden tools handling.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Modules: Plant Care, Soil Preparation, Landscaping Basics, Tool Maintenance
  • Schedule: Mondays and Wednesdays
  • Requirements: Basic gardening experience preferred but not required

What You Will Learn

  • How to care for and maintain various types of plants
  • Techniques for soil preparation and fertilization
  • Basic landscaping and garden design principles
  • Proper use and maintenance of gardening tools
