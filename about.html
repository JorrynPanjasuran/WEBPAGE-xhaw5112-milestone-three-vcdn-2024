Empowering the Nation

Our Story

Empowering the Nation is a local initiative founded in 2018 by Precious Radebe with the mission to provide essential skills training to domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Born out of a desire to uplift members of her community, Precious created this initiative to help individuals, like her parents, who were never given the opportunity to upskill themselves. Through the training programs, domestic workers and gardeners are given a chance to improve their job prospects or start their own businesses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, professional training that empowers domestic workers and gardeners with marketable skills, allowing them to secure better employment or start their own small businesses. We offer a variety of six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes, designed to meet the growing demand for skilled workers in households and gardens across the region. At Empowering the Nation, we believe that upskilling can change lives, and we are committed to helping every learner unlock their potential.

Our Courses

We offer the following six-month courses:

  • First Aid
  • Sewing
  • Landscaping
  • Life Skills

Our six-week short courses include:

  • Child Minding
  • Cooking
  • Garden Maintenance

Each course provides practical, hands-on training that prepares learners for real-world tasks and employment opportunities. Discounts are available when customers select more than one course, making it easier to upskill across multiple areas at a reduced cost.
