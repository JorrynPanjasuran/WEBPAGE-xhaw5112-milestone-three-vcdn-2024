Cooking Course

Cooking Course Overview

The Cooking Course provides learners with practical culinary skills for home or professional environments. Perfect for aspiring chefs, housekeepers, or anyone wanting to improve their cooking skills, this course covers everything from basic cooking techniques to meal presentation.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Modules: Basic Cooking Techniques, Meal Planning, Food Safety, and Presentation
  • Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays
  • Requirements: None, open to all skill levels

What You Will Learn

  • Essential cooking methods and techniques
  • How to plan and prepare nutritious meals
  • Food safety practices and kitchen hygiene
  • Plating and meal presentation for professional settings
