Empowering the Nation

Upskill Yourself with Our 6-Month Courses

Empowering the Nation offers a range of six-month courses designed to enhance the skills of domestic workers and gardeners, making them more marketable and helping them earn higher wages or become entrepreneurs.

Landscaping Course

Explore the principles of landscape design, including plant selection and hardscaping, to create stunning outdoor environments.

Life Skills Course

Develop critical life skills such as financial management, communication, and problem-solving to navigate everyday challenges.

Sewing Course

From basic stitches to garment construction, this course covers machine sewing, alterations, and custom clothing design.

First Aid Course

Learn essential first aid techniques to respond to medical emergencies and provide care in critical situations.
